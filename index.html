Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HeyDude Wally Sox Classic

HeyDude shoes generally run true to size, but we recommend going up half a size if you prefer a looser fit or have wider feet. The Wally Sox Classic features a relaxed fit design with stretch-knit upper that adapts to your foot shape. If you're between sizes, we suggest choosing the larger size for optimal comfort.

Yes! HeyDude Wally Sox Classic shoes are machine washable. Remove the insoles and laces, then wash on a gentle cycle with cold water. Air dry only - do not put them in the dryer as heat can damage the materials. This makes maintenance easy and keeps your shoes fresh.

The Wally Sox Classic shoes weigh approximately 5.5 oz (156 grams) each, making them incredibly lightweight. This ultra-light design reduces foot fatigue and makes them perfect for all-day wear, travel, or any activity where comfort is priority.

Flex & Fold Technology allows the shoe to bend and flex naturally with your foot movement. The outsole is designed with strategic flex grooves that enable the shoe to fold and compress, making them highly packable for travel and providing natural foot movement during walking.

Yes, the elastic laces are adjustable and part of HeyDude's Easy-On System. You can adjust the tension to your preference and then slip the shoes on and off without retying. The elastic material provides a secure fit while maintaining comfort throughout the day.

The upper is made from bi-component stretch-knit material for flexibility and breathability. The interior features stretch-cotton lining for comfort, while the insole is removable memory foam with leather lining. The outsole is made from lightweight EVA material for durability and comfort.
